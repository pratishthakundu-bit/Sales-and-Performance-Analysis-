# Sales-and-Performance-Analysis-
Comprehensive analysis of multi-city fast-food sales data to identify top products, revenue drivers, and customer preferences with actionable business insights and recommendations.
## Project Overview

The analysis focuses on identifying:
- The top 3 best-selling products
- The product generating the highest revenue
- The best-performing city and manager
- The most preferred payment and purchase types
- Strategic recommendations for business growth

## Dataset

The sales data used for analysis is available in the Google Sheet:  
[Sales Data Google Sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ZsQlFj7vBqjYalxRvZY-rS551gm_eWCT81KozJGat_c/edit?usp=sharing)

## The dataset includes:
- **Order ID**: Unique identifier for each transaction
- **Date**: Date of the transaction
- **Product**: Food category sold (e.g., Burgers, Fries, Beverages)
- **Price**: Unit price of each product
- **Quantity**: Number of units sold
- **Purchase Type**: Sales channel (Online, In-store, Drive-thru)
- **Payment Method**: Mode of payment (Credit Card, Gift Card, Cash)
- **Manager**: Manager responsible for the sale
- **City**: Location of the sale

## Key Findings

- Beverages, Fries, and Burgers are the top-selling products.
- Burgers generate the highest revenue, surpassing other products by a large margin.
- Lisbon city and manager Joao Silva lead in overall sales performance.
- The predominant payment method is Credit Card, with Online and In-store channels most used.
- Recommendations include focusing on burger combo offers, enhancing online ordering, and introducing a digital loyalty program.

## How to Use

- Review the Google Sheets data for detailed sales transactions.
